Explore what's lost: X's archive of Russian Information Operations in North Africa

Using the site: Click on any bar or row to explore more, including co-occurring users, hashtags, times of day, and matching tweets.

About this dataset: On March 17, 2021, X announced that it had removed a network of accounts, pages, and groups linked to Russian military intelligence services that targeted countries in North Africa. The operation was part of a broader effort to disrupt the activities of the Russian military intelligence services, which have been accused of interfering in the internal affairs of other countries.

The dataset provided contains tweets from the accounts that were part of the operation. The tweets were collected between January 1, 2020, and March 17, 2021, and include information about the content of the tweets, the accounts that posted them, and the engagement they received.
